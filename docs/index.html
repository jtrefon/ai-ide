The Mission

Stop the Bloat.

Modern AI, Native Speed

Most AI tools today are web browsers wearing a suit. They consume gigabytes of RAM before you even type a character. osx-ide uses the power of your Mac Pro, Studio, or MacBook to its full potential.

Full Ownership

By focusing on local-first workflows like local RAG and MLX-powered generation, we give you back control over your most sensitive IP: your source code.
